The importance of Values

Values are bounded with the vision of the company, it’s also about marketing and what the customer should feel about the company. A company define values, more like positive adjectives that serve the definition of the vision. It could show the ambition of the company or even the impact the company wants to have on the market or even on the environment for ecologic based companies.

-      Why do we need values? – For brand image, the promotion, to have a clear company vision, develop the culture of the company, attract customers and employees who share same values, achieve the vision of the company easily and in a better way. If you work in a company, you have to be able to share its values with other peoples. Basically this is the definition of the company.

-      How to give your employee the news values of the company?– To share new values, it’s crucial to organize projects and team works. For example, describing things that matter in their everyday work, talking in small groups and working in small teams. Creating a list of most popular values that came during the team work and implement values to employees, by involving people in the process for choosing values. Commitment will not be strong if values are only chosen in the board of director or by the CEO.

-      What are the benefits to have values?– Clear values are useful to define the company in an effective way and to guide the behaviour of employee toward the company and the public/customers. It works like a road or a guideline that allow you to describe in a proper way your strategy and main goals you want to achieve. You have to develop what there is behind generics worlds and adapt the definition to your company and explain why they collide with the company. They will help to make big decisions or to discuss. Employee have to be able to say, I feel those values during my day, with colleagues and customers. Never choose a value without describing it to your employees, it might be misunderstood.
